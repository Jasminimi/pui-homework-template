Paris Olympics: Taekwondo Design Project

Welcome to my project showcase for the Paris Olympics. This project focuses on integrating Taekwondo's rich heritage with the elegance of Paris, represented through graphic design. Below, you'll find my inspiration and final outputs, including posters, tickets, brochures, and more.

Inspiration

Every design begins with inspiration. For this project, I drew from diverse sources, including Korean culture, Taekwondo, traditional calligraphy, and the architectural beauty of France. These elements are showcased below in a diamond grid format.
